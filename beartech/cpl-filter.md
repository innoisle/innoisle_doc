#### **Why CPL Filters Matter for Comma Devices:**

CPL (Circular Polarizing Lens) filters work by **blocking polarized light**‚Äîthe kind often caused by reflections on glass surfaces like your windshield. These reflections can confuse vision-based systems like OpenPilot, especially when they obscure key features like lane lines or nearby vehicles.

By adding a CPL filter, you're giving your Comma device a cleaner, clearer view of the road ahead. This results in **better model confidence**, especially in high-sun or high-glare conditions.
